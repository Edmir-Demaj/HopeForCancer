About Hope for cancer

Our mission is to raise cancer awareness

Our mission is to increase cancer awareness and education, empower individuals to take control of their health, and provide support to those affected by cancer. We strive to do this by providing accessible and reliable information, fostering a sense of community and support, and promoting early detection and prevention.

Meet our Team

Welcome to HopeForCancer, behind this web app is Edmir Demaj, a Full Stack software development student at Code Institute. Edmir chose to create this project as part of his portfolio because he understands the sensitivity and importance of cancer awareness. Having lost his father to cancer in November of last year, Edmir is driven to raise awareness about this disease and provide resources for those who are affected by it. With HopeForCancer, our goal is to help individuals understand cancer better and empower them to make informed decisions about their health.
