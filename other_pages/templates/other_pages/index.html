What is cancer?

Cancer is a disease that occurs when abnormal cells in the body grow uncontrollably. These cells can form tumors or invade nearby tissues and spread to other parts of the body. While cancer can affect people of all ages, it is more common in older adults...

Types of cancer

There are many different types of cancer, including breast cancer, lung cancer, prostate cancer, and skin cancer. Each type of cancer has its own set of symptoms, risk factors, and treatment options. Learning about these different types of cancer can help you understand the disease better and take steps to reduce your risk.

Importance of early detection

Detecting cancer early is crucial to successful treatment and recovery. Regular cancer screenings and check-ups can help catch cancer in its early stages when it's easier to treat. Knowing the signs and symptoms of cancer can also help you detect the disease early and get prompt medical attention.
